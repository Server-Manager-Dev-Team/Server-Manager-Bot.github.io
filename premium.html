✨ Redeeming

Creating your Custom Branded Server Manager


note

We recommend prior knowledge of creating an application on the Discord Developer Portal in order to follow along with these steps. If you need any help, feel free to shoot us a message via our Support Bot (Helper#7371) and we can try and provide extra support.

1. Go to the Discord Developer Portal and click New Application

2. Give your bot a name, and click Create

3. Once you've completed those 2 steps, you should be on this page:

4. Click on the Bot tab on the left side of the screen. Then click Add Bot.

5. You can give it a name, change the Avatar, etc.

6. Navigate to where it says Reset Token, this is where you will be able to access your bot's token.

7. Once you complete the 2FA to reset your bot's token (if enabled), click Copy to copy it to your device's clipboard - you'll need this for later. Make sure to not share it with anyone else!

Enable Intents

1. Go to the Bot tab in your bot's application in the Developer Portal. Scroll down until you see the section: Privileged Gateway Intents.

2. Enable the following intents (the bot requires all of them):

  • Presence Intent
  • Server Members Intent
  • Message Content Intent

✅Success

You've created the bot application for your Custom Branded Server Manager bot!

Please make sure to message our support bot (Helper#7371) in our support server in order to redeem your custom bot, please also provide the token your have copied from earlier!
